Student Experience Survey

What Students Say

The full arc: a year-over-year jump in climate (Fall ’24 → Fall ’25, % favorable), then within-year detail on a 1–5 scale (Fall ’25 → Spring ’26).

The story of the year is belonging and energy: "energy of the school" (+14.1), "feel like you belong" (+12.3), and "students show respect" (+11.9) drove a 13-point jump in climate. The watch item is academic challenge — "work is appropriately challenging" fell 4.6 points.
